**Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)** capture a significant design decision,
the context behind it, and its consequences. They live in
[`docs/adr/`](https://github.com/calimero-network/core/tree/master/docs/adr) and
are the authoritative record of _why_ the system is the way it is — read the
relevant ADR before changing the subsystem it covers.

<Aside type="tip">
Proposing a design change? Add an ADR (copy the format of an existing one) so the
decision and its trade-offs are recorded alongside the code. Exploratory designs
live in [`docs/design/`](https://github.com/calimero-network/core/tree/master/docs/design).
</Aside>
